Workout Android App

My partner and I developed a android workout app. The app allowed the user to make an account and then login. The app then took them to the home screen where they generated a random workout plan. They then can select a specific exercise to view a demonstration of that workout. The user could save any generated workout and name it whatever they wanted. The user can also view different gyms in the Wilmington area.

Firebase was used to store user data including their email and password. Firebase was also used to store all the GIFs of the workouts.
